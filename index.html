üí° Business Insights & Strategic Recommendations
Data-Driven Findings and Actionable Strategies

üîç Key Discoveries

Discovery #1: Electrification is Most Cost-Effective

Analysis reveals that offshore electrification provides the highest ROI, delivering 50% emissions reduction for just $3.8B investment. This represents approximately $76M per percentage point of reduction - significantly better than other approaches.

Discovery #2: CCS Technology Gap

While ADNOC leads in CCS deployment, current capacity (800k tonnes/year) is only 8% of 2030 target. This 12x scale-up requirement in 6 years represents a significant execution risk requiring immediate acceleration.

Discovery #3: Methane Performance Excellence

ADNOC's methane intensity (0.07%) is already industry-leading, 85% below industry average. This positions them uniquely to achieve zero methane by 2030, creating competitive advantage and regulatory compliance ahead of peers.

Discovery #4: Green Hydrogen Timing Risk

Green hydrogen investments show lower near-term returns due to technology maturity. However, early positioning in this market could yield strategic advantages post-2035 as global demand accelerates.
